This Privacy Policy is a starting template tailored to an AI photo and video app. It is not legal advice. Please review and adapt it with qualified legal counsel before publishing, and replace every bracketed placeholder (for example [Company Name]) with your details.

This Privacy Policy explains how [Company Name] ("Sova", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Sova mobile application and related services (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

We aim to collect only what we need to operate the Service:

  • Content you provide. Photos or images you upload, text prompts you enter, and the videos or images you generate.
  • Purchase information. Records of subscriptions, credit purchases, and transaction status. Payments are processed by Apple; we do not receive your full payment card details.
  • Device and usage data. Device model, operating system version, app version, language, approximate region, crash logs, and basic analytics about how features are used.
  • Identifiers. A randomly generated app or device identifier used to associate your credits and creations with your installation.

The Service is designed to work without requiring you to create an account or provide your name, and we do not ask for more personal information than is necessary.

2. How we use information

  • To generate the videos and images you request.
  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service and its features.
  • To process purchases, manage credits, and prevent abuse or fraud.
  • To diagnose problems, monitor stability, and understand which features are useful.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. AI processing and third-party providers

Generating videos and images requires sending your prompts and uploaded photos to third-party artificial-intelligence and cloud infrastructure providers that perform the generation on our behalf. These providers process your content only to deliver the requested result and are bound by their own terms and privacy commitments. We do not sell your content.

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only:

  • With service providers (such as AI generation, cloud hosting, and analytics vendors) who process data on our behalf.
  • With Apple, for payments, subscriptions, and app distribution.
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of users, the public, or [Company Name].
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to this Policy.

5. Data retention and deletion

We retain your uploaded content and generated results for as long as needed to provide the Service, and for a limited period afterward to support troubleshooting and abuse prevention. You can delete your creations within the app. To request deletion of your data, contact us at litilabs@outlook.com.

6. Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal information, and to object to certain uses. To exercise these rights, contact us at litilabs@outlook.com. You can also manage subscriptions and purchases through your Apple ID settings.

7. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age required by your local law (for example, 13, or 16 in some regions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below that age. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Payments

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. Your payment details are handled by Apple under Apple's terms and privacy policy; we receive only the information needed to confirm and manage your purchase.

10. International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including by our service providers. Where required, we take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice within the app.

12. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at litilabs@outlook.com or [Company Name], [Company Address].
